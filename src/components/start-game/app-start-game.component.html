Rules

Each player has two decks of cards: Hero deck and Spaceship deck

The rules are:

  • Player 1 stars round and select deck to play
  • Player 1 select card from deck
  • Player 1 marks ready
  • Player 2 selects card
  • Player 2 marks ready
  • Player 1 and Player 2 reveal cards
  • Player 1 and Player 2 compare cards
  • Hero deck card comapre height
  • Spaceship deck cards comapre crew
  • Player with higher height or crew wins
  • Next round starts with draw missing card deck
  • Game is won by player who win more round
